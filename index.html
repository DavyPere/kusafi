Kusafi is an online platform for cleaning services. We offer laundry cleaning services and house cleaning services by linking you with the Best qualified, vetted and trained cleaning professional called SideKicks in a matter of minutes.

Go online to www.kusafi.co, select Pricing and in just a few taps, fill out a form requesting the service you need from the comfort of your home.

Can I Schedule for an appointment??

Yes you can. When requesting for a service, you will see a Calendar that allows you to select a date and time. A SideKick shall be at your specified location at the exact time and date scheduled.
